<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

The Swiggy Sales Dashboard is an interactive data analytics project that helps analyze food delivery sales performance.
It provides a complete view of total sales, average rating, order value, number of orders, and sales trends across different time periods, food types, cities, and states.
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer behavior and business performance easily.


<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

Food delivery platforms like Swiggy generate large amounts of daily order data.
Because of this, it becomes difficult to:
-Track overall sales and order performance<br>
-Understand customer ratings and preferences<br>
-Identify top-performing cities and states<br>
-Analyze daily, weekly, and monthly sales trends<br>

<h2><a class="anchor" id="dashboard"></a>Dashboard</h2>

The dashboard includes:<br>
-KPI cards displaying total sales, average rating, average order value, total orders, and rating count<br>
-Monthly, daily, and weekly sales trend analysis<br>
-Sales distribution by food type (Veg vs Non-Veg)<br>
-State-wise sales visualization using map charts<br>
-Top-performing cities based on sales<br>
-Interactive slicers for month, category, and restaurant selection<br><br>
